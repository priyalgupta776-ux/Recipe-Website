MacnCheese

Mac and Cheese Recipe (Easy & Creamy)** Ingredient * 1 cup elbow macaroni (or any pasta) * 1 tbsp butter * 1 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida) * 1 cup milk * ½–1 cup grated cheese (cheddar/mozzarella/processed – any is fine) * Salt (to taste) * Black pepper (to taste) * Optional: chilli flakes, oregano, garlic powder

Instructions 1. Boil the Pasta * Take water in a pot, add a pinch of salt and a few drops of oil. * Boil pasta for **7–8 minutes** until soft. * Drain and keep aside.

---

2. Make the Cheese Sauce * In a pan, melt **1 tbsp butter**. * Add **1 tbsp flour (maida)** and mix for 20–30 seconds on low flame. * Slowly add **1 cup milk** while whisking so no lumps form. * Cook until it thickens.

---

3. Add Cheese * Add the grated cheese. * Stir until melted and creamy. * Add **salt, pepper**, and optional seasonings.

---

4. Combine * Add the cooked pasta to the sauce. * Mix well until fully coated.

---

5. Serve * Enjoy hot! * You can add extra cheese and bake for 5 minutes if you want a crispy top.
