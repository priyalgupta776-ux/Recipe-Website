Plum Cake

Ingredient

* 1 cup all-purpose flour * 1 cup mixed dried fruits (plums, raisins, cherries, etc.) * ½ cup sugar * ½ cup butter * 2 eggs * ½ cup milk * 1 tsp baking powder * ½ tsp cinnamon powder * ½ tsp nutmeg powder * 1 tsp vanilla essence

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a cake tin and keep aside. 2. In a bowl, cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. 3. Add the eggs one by one and beat well. 4. Mix in vanilla essence. 5. In another bowl, combine flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and nutmeg. 6. Add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture slowly, mixing gently. 7. Add milk to adjust the batter consistency. 8. Fold in the chopped dried fruits. 9. Pour the batter into the greased tin. 10. Bake for 35–45 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. 11. Cool completely before slicing and serving.
